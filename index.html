Welcome to the Fantasy Football War Room League

Welcome to the official home of our fantasy football league! This site was created to bring all league members together in one place, making it easy to track teams, follow standings, and submit start/sit, waiver, or roster requests.

Whether you're a longtime vet or brand-new to fantasy football, this page will guide you through everything you can do on our site. Come explore current league teams, check updated weekly records, and submit those requests to win your weekly matchups! Please follow us on social media to help build our following.

What You Can Do Here

Our Teams page breaks down each franchise, showing team names, owners, and their history in the league. It's a great way to learn more about the league owners.

The Standings page allows you to track league rankings in real time. Wins, losses, and overall rank update weekly so you always know where owners stand in the race to win it all.

The Submissions page allows you to input your roster decisions to us. We allow you to input your league settings to provide us the information necessary to help you out. Our league experts will reply back to you within 24 hours!
